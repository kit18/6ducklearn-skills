# 6DuckLearn Trademark Guidelines

`6DuckLearn` is a product and brand name owned by its maintainers.

You may:

- use these skills with 6DuckLearn or compatible agent hosts
- describe forks as "for 6DuckLearn" or "compatible with 6DuckLearn"
- adapt the skills under the repository license

You may not:

- imply an unofficial fork is the official 6DuckLearn service
- use the 6DuckLearn name or logo in a confusing way
- publish private endpoints, credentials, or user data under the 6DuckLearn brand
