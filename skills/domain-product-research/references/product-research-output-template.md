# Product Research Output Template

Use this as a reference template, not a mandatory global format.

## 1. Research question
- What question are we trying to answer?
- What decision depends on the answer?

## 2. Executive read
- One-paragraph view of the product or company.
- Confidence level and biggest caveat.

## 3. Customer and problem
- Who is the customer?
- What pain point is being solved?
- How urgent and frequent is the problem?

## 4. Product wedge
- What is the narrow reason this product wins attention or usage?
- Is the wedge distribution, workflow, data, community, brand, or cost?

## 5. Competitor map
- Who are the closest substitutes?
- What does the target do better?
- What does the target do worse?

## 6. Moat and durability
- What advantage might persist?
- What is likely copied quickly?
- What would make the moat weaker over time?

## 7. Business and operating signals
- Revenue model and pricing shape.
- Unit economics signals if available.
- Adoption, retention, or engagement evidence if available.

## 8. Risks and failure modes
- What would make this a bad product thesis?
- What would make this a bad business?
- What evidence would disconfirm the current view?

## 9. Recommendation
- Proceed, monitor, or stop.
- What should we watch next?
