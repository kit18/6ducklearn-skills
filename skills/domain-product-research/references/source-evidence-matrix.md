# Source Evidence Matrix

Use the lightest evidence mix that can answer the question.

## Customer lane
- Reviews
- Community posts
- Support or complaint themes
- Interview notes

## Product lane
- Official docs
- Changelog
- Demo flows
- Pricing page

## Market lane
- Competitor websites
- Analyst writeups
- Search trend or category signals
- Public benchmarks

## Business lane
- Revenue or pricing clues
- Hiring patterns
- Partnerships
- Distribution channels

## Internal lane
- 6DuckLearn memory
- Prior session summaries
- Saved research notes
- Existing theses and decisions

## Reliability rule
- First-party sources explain intent.
- Third-party usage signals explain reality.
- Internal memory explains prior context.
- None of them alone is enough for a strong conclusion.
