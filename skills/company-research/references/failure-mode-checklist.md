# Failure Mode Checklist

Ask these before sounding confident.

## Business failure modes
- Growth was cyclical, not durable.
- Margins were peak-cycle, not normal.
- Management spent poorly.
- The moat is distribution-light and easy to copy.
- One product or customer mattered too much.

## Stock failure modes
- Valuation already assumes near-perfect execution.
- Event risk is close and underappreciated.
- The market is extrapolating temporary momentum.
- A narrative is substituting for evidence.

## Honesty rule
- If you cannot explain what would make this a terrible investment, you do not understand the investment well enough.
