# Important Dates Checklist

Treat dates as part of the investment process, not admin.

## Core dates to check
- Next earnings release.
- Next annual report filing.
- Annual meeting or shareholder vote.
- Ex-dividend date and dividend announcement when relevant.
- Lockup expiry, insider selling window, or major vesting event.
- Product launch or conference date.
- Regulatory decision or hearing date.
- Debt maturity or refinancing window.

## Questions to ask
- Which date could change the thesis?
- Which date only adds noise?
- Is the stock already pricing in the event?
- What happens if the event is merely fine instead of great?

## Practical rule
- Never call something a good entry without checking whether an important date is close enough to dominate the setup.
- Default source of truth: the integrated important-dates tool backed by `stock_events` and filing-derived fiscal anchors. Only fall back to ad hoc calendars when the tool reports incomplete coverage.
