# PKM Synthesis Template

Use this as a reference template, not a mandatory universal format.

## 1. Question
- What are we trying to synthesize?
- Why does it matter now?

## 2. Core themes
- What ideas recur across notes?
- Which themes look strong versus fragile?

## 3. Contradictions and tensions
- Where do the notes disagree?
- What changed over time?
- What tension is still unresolved?

## 4. What seems true right now
- Best current synthesis.
- Confidence level.
- Biggest caveat.

## 5. What should carry forward
- Decisions to keep.
- Principles to keep.
- Warnings to keep.

## 6. Open questions
- What still needs evidence?
- What needs a fresh decision?

## 7. Next action
- Save, revisit, test, or act.
