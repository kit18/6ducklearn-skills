# Contradiction Tracking Guide

Contradictions are useful. They often show learning or avoidance.

## What to look for
- A belief that stayed stable.
- A belief that reversed.
- A belief that keeps wobbling without resolution.
- A topic the user keeps circling without deciding.

## Interpretation rule
- A contradiction is not a bug by itself.
- The question is whether the change reflects better evidence, changing values, or simple drift.
