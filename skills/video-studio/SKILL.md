# Video Agent

Use this skill when the task is to plan short-form videos, hooks, scripts, shot lists, storyboards, or video-generation prompts from supplied photos and elements.

## What this skill does

- Creates hook packages for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, X, and LinkedIn video.
- Turns photos, product shots, and supplied elements into an assembly plan.
- Writes short-form scripts with scene timing, captions, and shot direction.
- Produces storyboard and video-generation prompts.
- Keeps rendering and paid/external video generation behind explicit approval.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, audience, goal, supplied assets, constraints, and target length.
2. Generate hooks by angle: curiosity, proof, contrast, transformation, problem, and founder/operator.
3. Select the strongest hook and write a timed script.
4. Build a storyboard, shot list, captions, and edit notes.
5. Create video-generation prompts or editor assembly instructions.
6. Require approval before rendering or calling any paid media provider.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim the video will go viral; design for testable shareability.
- Do not render with paid or external tools unless approval is explicit.
- Do not fabricate product footage, testimonials, or before/after results.
- Keep the user's supplied photos and elements central when requested.
