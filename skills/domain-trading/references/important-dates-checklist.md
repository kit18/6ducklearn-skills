# Trading Important Dates Checklist

Check these before acting on a setup.

- Next earnings date.
- Annual report or annual meeting timing.
- Product event or launch date.
- Regulatory or legal catalyst date.
- Dividend or ex-dividend date when relevant.
- Macro date that could overwhelm the stock-specific thesis.

## Questions
- Is the trade really about the business, or mainly about the date?
- Is the event already crowded in the price?
- What happens if the result is only decent?

## Default source
- Use the integrated important-dates tool first. It should reflect `stock_events` plus filing-derived fiscal anchors before you trust a separate calendar page or headline.
