# Annual Report Sanity Check

Before trusting a stock story, check the last annual report or latest full-year result.

## Look for
- Revenue quality, not just growth rate.
- Margin stability or deterioration.
- Cash flow support for the story.
- Dilution, debt, or aggressive capital allocation.
- Risk disclosures that became more serious.

## Rule
- If the filing and the narrative disagree, trust the filing first.
